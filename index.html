The Person Behind the Edit

About Michelle

Michelle Rupprecht is a Denver-based video editor and media producer with over a decade of experience crafting stories that connect, inform, and inspire. Her career spans an unusually wide range of formats — from intimate documentary portraits to the controlled chaos of live awards broadcasts.

As part of the NBCUniversal broadcast team, Michelle contributed to coverage of the Emmy Awards and Grammy Awards, working alongside teams who operate at the highest level of the industry. That experience sharpened her instincts for pacing, structure, and delivering polished work under unforgiving deadlines.

Beyond broadcast, Michelle has built a strong portfolio in brand storytelling and social content — partnering with organizations like the University of Colorado Boulder, the City of Boulder, BARE, Stahlsac, and Enercom. Whether the project calls for cinematic production value or scrappy creator energy, she knows how to make it work.
